The new bar is located right by the beautiful Kastellet park, near the city center. Look for a white flag with a blue circle – the little blue dot marks the spot!

The bar is open Tuesday-Saturday from 3:00 pm until 11:00 pm

We want to make sure that all our guests have a great time at our restaurant. That's why we operate on a walk-in basis and don't take reservations in advance. This allows us to provide a more flexible and spontaneous experience for everyone. Just come by whenever you're ready to enjoy our delicious wine, and we'll be happy to welcome you!
