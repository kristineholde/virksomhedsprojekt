LAPIS LAZULI

To awaken the senses, the ultramarine pigment before you is left open and exposed, free to express itself and interact with its surroundings, free to shine – much like the wines served

If touched, the pigment travels and leaves a mark, hence we kindly ask you, however tempting, not to touch the walls, but instead, among other necessary gestures, focus your hands on swirling the wine in the must-not-break fragile and featherlight glasses to release all the enticing aromas.
